About Me

I've been building websites and web applications for more than twenty years, and have gained an eye for design as well. I've specialized in accessibility over the past few years, helping clients meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.0 AA).

Recently I spent five years at one of the largest advertising agencies in Hawaii, Anthology Marketing Group. I worked in their digital group, collaborating with a small team of creatives and backend developers. I helped deliver fully responsive solutions which ranged from enterprise clients to small websites based on Wordpress or Drupal. I was able to work with clients in industries such as healthcare, finance, automotive, hospitality, and restaurants.

Prior to that, I worked with Hawaii International Film Festival, a non-profit, in a variety of roles starting with Information Technology and later moving into Email Marketing and Social Media. While there, I created a web application to manage the festival's extensive film and screening data (150+ films and 250+ screenings per festival). I later created native apps for iOS and Android so festival patrons could browse films & screenings, buy tickets and create their own schedule.

I have taken a position with SailPoint Technologies as of March 2020 working on their customer community website. I'm not currently available for freelancing.
