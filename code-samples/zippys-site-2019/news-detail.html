Support American Diabetes Association of Hawai‘i and Live Well

January 6, 2019 | Promos

Through the month of January, order a Korean Chicken Salad at any of our Zippy’s locations and you will be supporting the American Diabetes Association of Hawai‘i! A portion of the proceeds from each Korean Chicken Salad sold will be donated to the American Diabetes Association of Hawai‘i! There are other ways you can show your support, like purchasing a cutout for a $1. Ask your server for details.

This campaign ends January 31st, but to live well takes a lifetime! So get a fresh start this year and make your next stop, Zippy’s.

Zippy’s and the American Diabetes Association continue to bring you healthier meal options for people with prediabetes, diabetes, or those interested in living healthier. Regular physical activity and healthier food choices are an important part of maintaining your health. When eating out we want you to remember these important tips:

  • 1. Plan ahead.
  • 2. Think about portions.
  • 3. Choose meats that are lower in saturated and trans fat.
  • 4. Add fruits, vegetables and whole grains.
  • 5. Choose your beverages carefully. Opt for water instead of sugar-sweetened beverages.

Do you have prediabetes or diabetes?

Not everyone with prediabetes or diabetes [type 1 or type 2] has the same meal plan or nutritional goals. For many, cutting carbohydrates and calories is most important. The ADA and Kaiser Permanente encourages you to work with your health care team to identify you individualized goals. For more information, please visit us at www.diabetes.org/hawaii,  or call (808) 947-5979. Like us on Facebook at www.facebook.com/adahawaii.

…And remember, include physical activity as part of your day! Being physically active improves your blood sugar levels and keeps your heart healthy.
